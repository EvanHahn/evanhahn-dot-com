Madman

- When an intellectual (madman) goes crazy, he has access to multiple points of view. He has a place to explore his madness. He has leisure time. He has help. He's a man. So he does okay, eventually.
- When a woman goes crazy, she has nothing and she is spiritually tainted. She's not a privileged man. So she dies.

- In both stories, there's a sharp critique of tradition
- You couldn't beat tradition; pessimistic stories
